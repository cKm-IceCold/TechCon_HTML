History

TechCon started in 2020 when four great minds came together to create this event. They saw the immense benefits of bringing together techies from across Africa to share ideas, build solutions, and address pressing challenges.

Mission

TechCon 2024 is dedicated to empowering young and aspiring tech enthusiasts to break into the industry and become Africa’s next generation of innovators. Through seminars, workshops, and networking opportunities, we are driving collaboration that transforms lives and accelerates Africa’s growth.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed world-class innovators who have shaped the tech landscape in Africa. One of our most notable speakers is Mr. Emmanuel Paul Chukwuma, a pioneer of blockchain innovation in Africa’s fintech space. He has been a keynote speaker at all three past editions of TechCon, inspiring thousands of tech professionals and entrepreneurs.
